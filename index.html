My Approach

I am known for my openness, active engagement, honesty, collaboration, consistency, patience, and self-compassion. I enter therapy with an open mind and a willingness to explore the challenges, seeking new perspectives and solutions. During therapy sessions, I actively participate by sharing your thoughts, emotions, and experiences. I approach therapy with honesty, being truthful with my patients.
